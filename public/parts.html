Overview

This year, among the 89 parts designed, 65 of them are basic parts and the rest are composite parts. All of them are designed by ourselves, and most of them have been tested to insure their reliability. You can click here to learn more about our project to see their specific functions, or click on the part numbers when browse website.

Basic Parts

Among the 65 basic parts designed this year, BBa_K5136003 is our favorite one that codes CYP199A4 T253A-His tag. Among all of monooxygenases we tested, the monooxygenase encoded by this gene has the best deinking effect. This monooxygenase can exhibit excellent performance in related research conducted by other researchers and iGEM teams. Besides, many other basic parts have also been designed to construct our project.

Composite Parts

We have designed a series of composite parts this year, and most of them could be useful tools for our design. Our favorite composite part is BBa_K5136202. This composite part demonstrates that the combination of the promoter J23104 and the signal peptide LMT can achieve the best secretion effect, providing strong support for using signal peptide to obtain the deinking enzymes in our project.
