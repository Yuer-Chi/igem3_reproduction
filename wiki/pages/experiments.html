CspA mRNA Experiment

Requirements

● LB liquid medium
● Tecan Infinite M200 microplate reader

Protocol

1. Revive the transformed bacteria by incubating them at 37 °C with shaking at 200 r.p.m. for 12 hours.
2. Inoculate the revived bacteria at 2% concentration into 10 mL of LB medium and shake at 37 °C for around 2 hours until the OD600 values reaches 0.6-0.8.
3. Incubate the above bacteria separately at 4 °C, 15 °C, and 37 °C, each with shaking at 200 r.p.m. .
4. Use Tecan Infinite M200 microplate reader to measure the green fluorescence intensity and OD600 values at 2 hours, 4 hours, and 6 hours.
Note: Excitation wavelength: 488 nm, Emission wavelength: 530 nm.
5. Calculate the ratio of green fluorescence intensity to OD600 data.
