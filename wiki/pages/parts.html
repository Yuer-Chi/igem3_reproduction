Overview

This year, among the 79 parts designed, 57 of them are basic parts and the rest are composite parts. All of them are designed by ourselves, and most of them have been tested to insure their reliability. You can click here to learn more about our project to see their specific functions, or click on the part numbers when browse website.

Basic Parts

Among the 57 basic parts designed this year, BBa_K5136003 is our favorite one that codes CYP199A4 T253A-his tag. Among all of monooxygenases we tested, the monooxygenase encoded by this gene has the best deinking effect. This monooxygenase can exhibit excellent performance in related research conducted by other researchers and iGEM teams. Besides, many other basic parts have also been designed to construct our project.
